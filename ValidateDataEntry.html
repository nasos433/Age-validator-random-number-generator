Age checker for asmission

This will check if the age is 0 - 18 the person cant come in
If they are 18 or over but less than 21 they can come in but not drink
if they are 21 or over they can come in and drink

The next part is to alert the fine for breach of this rule
It takes the age of the person and multiplies it by 20 and adds 100 if they are under 18
It takes the age of the person and multiplies it by 22 if they are 18 - 21
